Features

Built for the whole writing process

From first draft to final line, Ilena gives you the tools to write more precisely — in your own voice.

Lyric editor

Write and organise all your works in one place. Your songs, poems, and drafts are stored securely and accessible from any device.

Definition dictionary

Look up any word without leaving your work. Understand nuance and choose the word that means exactly what you intend.

Rhyme suggestions

Find the right rhyme — perfect, near, or slant — without compromising what you're trying to say.

Rhythm & syllable analysis

See syllable counts and stress patterns line by line. Keep your metre consistent without losing what makes a line yours.

Synonym & antonym tool

Explore alternatives for any word — sharpen imagery, avoid repetition, or find a better fit for the line.

Language refinement

Identify awkward phrasing and grammatical inconsistencies — without losing the natural quality of your voice.

Style analysis

Considered feedback on tone, imagery, and structure — observations that strengthen your craft without overriding your voice.

How it works

Write. Analyse. Refine.

Ilena fits into your creative process — not the other way around.

01

Write your draft

Open a project and start writing. No templates, no constraints — a blank canvas for your ideas.

02

Get instant feedback

Ilena analyses your work as you write, surfacing rhythm issues and suggesting rhymes and definitions in context.

03

Refine on your terms

Browse alternatives and suggestions. Accept what serves the work, ignore what doesn't. You remain the author.

04

Save & return

Your works are stored in the cloud. Pick up where you left off from any device, at any time.
